# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Gen-Z / late-millennial shoppers at the moment of purchase. Context: high-scroll mood, mobile-first, TikTok-adjacent brands. Often buying gaming merch, gen-Z beauty, low-rise drops, music merch, subscription beauty. Emotional goal: match the same high-color energy as the storefront — the checkout should feel like part of the launch, not a gray step-down.

### Brand Personality
Three concrete words: **bright, bouncy, nostalgic-future**.

Voice: the early-web nostalgia of a Y2K fashion magazine crossed with a Dreamcast UI. Confident, colorful, never ironic — it's not cosplay, it's the real pop.

### Aesthetic Direction
Y2K / retro-arcade checkout. Think: Tamagotchi-era gradients + iMac G3 translucent blues + Pokémon stat cards + 2003-era anime UI + jelly candy. Chunky pill buttons, soft-inner-glow on selected chips, lightly-bouncy motion.

- **Fonts**: Migra or Reckless Neue for display (wide + confident), General Sans or Satoshi for body, JetBrains Mono for numerics. NOT Inter / Poppins / default Figma body fonts.
- **Palette**: soft lavender `#E9DCFF` primary surface, hot peach `#FF9B8F` as the Y2K gloss accent, electric blue `#3A5BFF` as the "pop" secondary, deep plum `#2A1F4D` for type. One sparkle-soft-gradient `linear-gradient(135deg, #FF9B8F, #E9DCFF 50%, #3A5BFF)` — used once (on the page bg or the CTA) but not twice.
- **No**: flat gray, drop shadows with dark colors, Instagram-gradient CTAs, bland neutrals, "brat-coded" lime unless it's neon's job.
- **Yes**: pill buttons (24–32px radius), soft inner glows via 0–2px white inner border, chunky rounded cards (16px), slight wiggle on hover (140ms), holographic-shimmer on primary CTA border, scanline-stamp step markers.

### Anti-references
- Stripe / Linear / Vercel
- Corporate SaaS "Notion-pastel"
- Over-saturated CMYK kitsch
- Airbnb-era rounded-but-beige

### Design Principles
1. **Color is the signal.** Three strong hues plus deep plum text. Neutrals are tinted lavender, never gray.
2. **Roundness has range.** CTAs fully pill (999px), cards 16px, inputs 12px. Never a single "safe" radius.
3. **The gradient is rare.** Used once as hero brand gesture (page bg seam or primary CTA border), never on two elements on the same viewport.
4. **Motion is bouncy but disciplined.** 140ms with a soft `cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)` (overshoot). Max 2px overshoot, no looping animations.
5. **Numbers are mono.** Prices and step markers stay JetBrains Mono so the system still reads as competent, not cartoon.
