# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
High-intent luxury shoppers at the moment of purchase. Context: measured attention, aspirational mood, often late-evening desktop or curated mobile. They are buying something they're allowed to want. Emotional goal: reassure and flatter — make the spend feel refined, not impulsive.

### Brand Personality
Three concrete words: **quiet, considered, boutique**.

Voice: like a hand-written bill from a Milanese tailor. Warm authority. Never "we've got your back" — always the house's quiet competence.

### Aesthetic Direction
Luxury boutique / atelier checkout. Think: Hermès orange (as restraint, not as color), Aesop apothecary labels, Loro Piana catalog, the heavy cardstock in a Galeries-Lafayette gift box.

- **Fonts**: Tiempos Display (or Canela, PP Editorial New — any Italian-serif high-contrast display) for titles, Inter-Tight / Söhne / Manrope for body (restraint, not character), JetBrains Mono for prices (slabs lock up the receipt). Explicitly not Inter default / DM Serif / Playfair (over-used) / Cormorant.
- **Palette**: deep forest green `#1F2A22` as brand ink, warm cream `#F5F1E8` surface, aged gold `#C7A15C` as the single accent (CTA, selected states only). Bronze-grey neutrals with hints of green chroma.
- **No**: pure white, pure black, drop shadows on cards, gradients, glowing accents, round pill buttons, neon anything, emoji in copy, "🔥 Free shipping" ticker bars.
- **Yes**: 2px radius (almost-square), 1px hairline in warm bronze, serif display for all headings with tight tracking, generous letter-spacing on eyebrows (`0.22em`), tabular-nums for every price, 8/12/16/24/40 spacing scale, double-hairline rules under section titles.

### Anti-references
- Shopify default modern boutique templates (too-rounded, too-many-cards)
- Every DTC skincare checkout from 2023 (pastel serif + Inter)
- Stripe indigo CTAs
- Anything that looks like a gym or a wellness startup

### Design Principles
1. **Restraint is the signal.** Empty space is the frame. Never decorate for decoration.
2. **Every price is mono and tabular.** Receipts must line up. Totals are bold, items are regular.
3. **Gold is the one accent.** It appears only on CTAs, selection states, and step markers. The rest is ink on cream.
4. **Display serif titles set the tone.** Body is silent; headings are the only typography that "talks".
5. **2px radius, 1px hairline, 120ms transitions.** Almost square. No bounce. No glow.
